About Alternator

This article explains, in brief, the information about various aspects of a car's alternator like what it is, what is its job, how it works and of course signs and symptoms whether it needs repairing?

The Alternator is also a kind of a generator

• In past it was also known as a generator. It functioning is also much the same, when car is running

• When a car is in motion it runs its wheels and in turn as a crank mechanism it turns a wheel on the alternator, this process generates power

• Take example of a hydrolytic power station. Here also water falls on spinning wheels, this turns the wheels and produces electricity

Provides electricity to the car and charges its battery also:

• It is a wrong belief that battery supplies the electricity in the car. It only provides electricity to starter motor to start the car. When car is moving the car alternator generates energy to be supplied to the car’s entire electric mechanism and also charges the battery


The history of Car Alternator

• Even during the second world war they used on the front. They were kept in special vehicles for supplying power to radio communication devices

• In cars they were first used by Chrysler in its Valiant model in 1960

Signs of a failing car alternator

• A warning light signal which looks like a battery or shows a caption “GEN” OR “ALT”, it’s a clear warning sign. The light may remain switched on, come on only when other electrical gadgets are in use in the car

• If in case warning light does not show there are some other danger signs also like, dim lights, car gauges and instruments suddenly stop working, if a belt is burning you can feel the smell of burning rubber, one can also experience strange sparking sounds under the car hood. All this signs are suggestive of dangerous situation of your car alternator

The confusion : battery or the car alternator

In a car’s electricity network, the alternator and battery are very closely connected. So, it becomes confusing to diagnose whether the alternator or battery is responsible for the trouble. There is an easy test for getting rid of this confusion, start the car now remove the jumper cables. If the car engines get switched off immediately it most likely because of the alternator. If still the car keeps on running the culprit is mostly the battery, it mostly needs recharging.
